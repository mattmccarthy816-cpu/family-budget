What's inside

Built for how
households actually work.

Not just a spreadsheet. A full financial operating system designed for the way real families manage money together.

📊
Track spending together
Multiple members, shared visibility. Log entries from anywhere — the dashboard updates in real time. Category budgets, pacing alerts, and a full entry history.
🎯
Plan for long-term goals
Savings accounts, investment portfolios, mortgage paydown, debt payoff. Each goal type gets the right math — compound growth projections, pacing markers, what-if simulators.
📈
Understand your trends
12 months of spend history visualized automatically. See which categories are trending up or down, your best and worst months, and how this month compares to last.
💬
Monthly review summaries
A plain-English summary of how you did — highest usage categories, biggest single spend, member split, and a forward projection. No dashboards to interpret.
🔮
Month-end projections
See where you're tracking before the month ends. A projected over/under updates daily as you log spend. Catch problems early, not at month-end.
⚙️
Built for your household
Configurable members with roles, custom categories with types (expense, fixed, investment), collapsible sections — designed to fit your household's structure, not a generic template.
Getting started

Up and running
in minutes.

No spreadsheet setup, no complex onboarding. Sign in, add your budget, start logging.

01
Sign in with Google
One tap — your account is your Google identity. No passwords, no extra setup. Your data lives in your own Google Sheet.
02
Set your budgets
Add categories, set monthly budgets, assign sections. Mark fixed expenses and investments separately so pacing logic works correctly from day one.
03
Log and track
Tap + Add after every spend. The dashboard updates immediately. Check in weekly to see trends, monthly to read your summary.
